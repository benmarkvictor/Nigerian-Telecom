# Nigerian Telecom Customer Analytics Dashboard

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes customer revenue, subscription behavior, and churn across Nigeria's telecommunications industry. By transforming raw telecom data into meaningful visualizations, the dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators, identify customer trends, evaluate regional performance, and make data-driven decisions that improve customer retention and business growth.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of telecom operations by highlighting revenue performance, customer activity, subscription preferences, network complaints, and churn distribution across multiple states. It is designed to help business leaders identify opportunities, address operational challenges, and optimize customer experience.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- **Power BI** (Data Modeling, Interactive Dashboard Development & Data Visualization)
- **Power Query** (Data Cleaning and Transformation)
- **DAX** (Calculated Measures and Business Metrics)
- **Microsoft Excel** (Data Preparation)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- The company generated a **total revenue of ₦42.87M**, demonstrating strong business performance across multiple states.

- Customers consumed an **average of 24.89 GB** of data, providing valuable insights into customer usage patterns and service demand.

- The dashboard recorded **777 active customers** and **823 churned customers**, indicating that customer retention remains a significant business challenge.

- **Postpaid subscribers** contributed approximately **54.1%** of total subscription revenue, while **Prepaid subscribers** accounted for **45.9%**, making Postpaid customers the largest revenue contributors.

- Approximately **51.64%** of churned customers reported network-related complaints, suggesting that service quality has a direct impact on customer retention.

- **Delta State** generated the highest monthly revenue, followed by **Enugu** and **Abuja**, making these regions key revenue drivers for the business.

- Delta also recorded the highest customer churn, highlighting the need to improve customer satisfaction even in the company's strongest-performing markets.

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

- Improve network infrastructure and service reliability in regions with high complaint rates to reduce customer churn.

- Develop targeted retention campaigns for high-value customers, particularly within Delta, Enugu, and Abuja.

- Introduce customer loyalty programs, personalized offers, and competitive data plans to improve customer retention.

- Continuously monitor customer complaints to identify recurring service issues and prioritize network improvements.

- Leverage predictive analytics to identify customers at risk of leaving and proactively engage them with personalized retention strategies.

- Expand marketing efforts in high-performing regions while improving customer experience in areas with elevated churn.

- Analyze customer usage behavior regularly to optimize pricing strategies and introduce data plans that better align with customer needs.
